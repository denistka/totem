## Technical Details

### Popup Auto-Pan Configuration

Leaflet `autoPanPadding` format: `[x, y]` where:
- `x` = horizontal padding (left/right)
- `y` = vertical padding (top/bottom)

Final configuration: `autoPanPadding={[20, 140]}`
- Small horizontal padding (20px) for edge clearance
- Large vertical padding (140px) accounts for header (~60px) and footer (~80px)

### Map Bounds Strategy

Attempted approaches that didn't work:
- `paddingTopLeft` / `paddingBottomRight` on MapContainer (only affects fitBounds, not marker rendering)
- CSS `clip-path` (clips entire map, not just markers)
- `maxBounds` restrictions (too restrictive for manual panning)

Final approach: Increased `FitBounds` padding to keep markers away from edges during auto-zoom.
